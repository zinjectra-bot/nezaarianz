About

A batch, a story, a family.

Nezaarianz is the proud batch of GUPS Paramba — a group forged through years of shared classrooms, laughter, and dreams.

GUPS Paramba

Since 1974, GUPS Paramba has nurtured generations of learners in the heart of Paramba — building character, curiosity, and community.

Nezaarianz

We are the storytellers of our own chapter — a batch bound not just by a classroom, but by inside jokes, late-night talks, and unshakable friendship.

Our Mission

To carry the spirit of GUPS Paramba wherever we go — to keep our bonds alive, celebrate our journey, Be Respectfull To The teachers, Elders, Childrens — and remind every Nezaarian: you're never alone in this story.
